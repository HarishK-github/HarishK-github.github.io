How to use:


1) Enter and seperate all the forces and angles in the respective boxes with a comma(,)


2) Enter angles w.r.t +ve x-axis in anti-clockwise direction


3) Enter direction as +1 if force rotates in anti-clockwise dir. w.r.t given point


4) Enter direction as -1 if force rotates in clockwise dir. w.r.t given point


5) Enter distance in meters w.r.t the mentioned point(given point)
